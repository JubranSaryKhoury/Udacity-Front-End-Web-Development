    France Section

    The French national football team, known as "Les Bleus," has a storied history in the UEFA European Championship, showcasing a blend of flair, tactical sophistication, and memorable performances. France has won the UEFA European Championship twice, in 1984 and 2000, marking significant milestones in their footballing legacy.
